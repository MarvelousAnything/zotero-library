      Biden Has ‘Great Meeting’ With European Leaders: Ukraine Update

      • EU foreign ministers met in Brussels to talk Russia response
      • Blinken joined the meeting by video to discuss Ukraine
      By

      John Follain and

      Alberto Nardelli

      January 24, 2022, 7:26 AM UTCUpdated onJanuary 24, 2022, 10:45 PM UTC

      Share this article

      Copied
      Follow the authors
      @JohnFollain
      + Get alerts for
      @albertonardelli
      + Get alerts for

      U.S. President Joe Biden held what he called a “great” call with European leaders, including French President Emmanuel Macron and German Chancellor Olaf Scholz, as western nations work to strike a unified position on Russia. 

      The North Atlantic Treaty Organization said it would boost its deployments in eastern Europe in a bid to deter a new Russian invasion in Ukraine as the Pentagon announced it’s put 8,500 troops on heightened alert. Russian President Vladimir Putin has denied he’s planning an attack.
